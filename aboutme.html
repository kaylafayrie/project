About Me

Hi, my name is Kayla Redman and I am an undergraduate student at Saint Mary's College of California. I am getting my Bachelor's of Arts in Communication. I want to get minors in Digital Studies and Sociology.

I am originally from Fremont, CA. I was born and raised in the Bay Area, and I plan to stay here. I am a Bay Area girl at heart. I love the weather here and the diverse culture.
